Tongji University is a junior industrial design student, but I don't think there is anything worthy of output for the time being. He may be interested in photography and travel photography. He spends a lot of time on meaningful and meaningless thinking every day. He is indecisive, but he thinks he is an activist who is the same in appearance and in deed. I've seen countless films and like to write film reviews. If you travel, you like mountains, rivers and nature, and live in a modern prosperous city (or Downtown).
